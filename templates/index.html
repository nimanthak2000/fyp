Project Overview

Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects millions of people worldwide. Early detection and management are crucial in preventing complications and improving quality of life for those affected. Our project addresses this challenge by leveraging machine learning techniques to predict the likelihood of an individual developing diabetes based on various factors such as Glucose, Insulin, BMI, and Age.

How it Works

Our system utilizes a dataset containing health-related features of individuals, including both diabetic and non-diabetic cases. We have trained a machine learning model on this dataset, employing algorithms including logistic regression and SVM to accurately predict the probability of diabetes occurrence for a given set of input parameters.
